About WarcraftLFG
WarcraftLFG strive to be a link between players and guilds: Our goal is to provide each party with a meaningful set of data to help you find your perfect guild, and your perfect members.

Our data comes from various source, and we are truly thankful for the quality of data provided by website such as WarcraftLogs and WoWProgress.

We felt that raiders and guilds alike were lacking a better way to find each other.

WarcraftLFG is our answer.
Guilds :
Tired of spamming the general chat with your recruitment macro? So were we.

WarcraftLFG is a simple and intuitive tool to find your next guildmate in just a few clicks. Our database contains hundreds -and growing! - of potential candidates and our goal is to help you connect with them.
Raiders :
Your experience is invaluable. Hundreds of guilds are desperate to find you. WarcraftLFG helps you connect with them in a simple and efficient way.
